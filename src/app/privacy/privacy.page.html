Privacy Policy

Personal data

Histamine Tracker does not collect any personal data. No personal data is needed for the app to work. If no personal data is collected, no data can be stolen, leaked or abused. This is the best way to protect the privacy of the app user.

App permissions

For the app to function properly a couple of permissions are needed:

  • Internet permission - to be able to access the OpenFoodFacts database for the ingredients of scanned products.
  • Camera permission - to be able to use the device camera for the barcode scans.

These permissions can be limited to app runtime and are only used for the described purpose.

Cookies

Histamine Tracker does not use any tracking or identifying cookies.

Privacy policies of other websites

The Histamine Tracker app contains links to other websites. This privacy policy applies only to Histamine Tracker application, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

Histamine Tracker keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this page. This privacy policy was last updated on 23 June 2022.
